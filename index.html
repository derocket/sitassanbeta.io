SIT ASSAN BETA

Your all-in-one college app! Designed to provide easy access to various college facilities, SIT ASSAN BETA aims to simplify your college experience. In its current beta version, the app offers access to the college's ERP system and online study materials, making your academic journey more convenient.

Download on Play Store

Key Features

  • ERP Integration: Seamlessly access your college's ERP system through SIT ASSAN BETA. Check your timetable, review grades, manage your academic profile, and more with ease.
  • Todo List: Stay organized and manage your tasks effectively with the built-in todo list feature. Keep track of assignments, deadlines, and other important responsibilities, ensuring you never miss a beat.
  • Write Notes: Take notes directly within the app, allowing you to jot down important information and capture ideas on the go. Your notes are securely stored and accessible whenever you need them.
  • SIC Barcode: We've introduced a convenient way to access campus facilities and services. Generate a barcode of your Student Identification Card (SIC) within the app, eliminating the need for physical cards and simplifying ID verification processes.
  • SIT HUB Access: Get access to SIT HUB, an innovative mobile application designed to streamline and enhance your academic journey. Access up-to-date study materials and ebooks to support your learning experience.
